Mission Statement

Flower, Itty Bitty Kitty Committee President

The Rabbit Haven was formed to achieve the following goals:

To educate the public all about rabbits, including their care, diet, grooming, medical and social needs.

To provide rescue, shelter, medical care, foster care, and spay/neuter services for abandoned and endangered rabbits.

To place rabbits into permanent, safe, loving, indoor homes.

About our Founder

Heather Bechtel

Heather Bechtel, Founder and Director of The Rabbit Haven, began working with rabbits and other animals in 1958.

Her family was involved in the medical field and provided much needed help and education to her in these early years. Heather has been involved in animal care (especially medical care) and rescue since that time.
